Life Aspirations

The names and descriptions of my biggest ongoing projects:

  • Fostering Flourishing: contributions to the global discussion on "the good life" and "well-being".
  • Learning to Love: programs and support for learning and training the skills of loving others and one's self.
  • Connection Cultivation: community events designed to facilitate new connections and conversations.
  • Actualization Aspiration: local events for personal passion project presentations.
  • Celebration Ceremonies: events for recognizing amazing contributions of people and projects globally and locally.
  • Idea Architecture: tools for information design that structure ideas in a more accessible and understandable way.
  • Information Curation: limited repositories of only the most useful scientific knowledge from a few experts per topic.
  • [Making Stuff]: creating music, comedy, videos, apps, tools, games, etc that I think should exist.
